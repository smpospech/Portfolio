College Station, TX

Location

College Station is located around 90 miles from Houston, and it is home of Texas A&M University.

About

Weather

The weather in College Station is very similar to the weather in Houston, except, there is less rainfall, because it is farther from the coast.

Population

The population is around 121,000, most of which are Aggies.

Sports

Aggieland is the home of Texas A&M Atheletics, most noteably being Texas A&M Football; however, it is also home to a beautiful golf course.

Things To Do

  • Play Golf
  • Go to Bars
  • Attend a Texas A&M atheletic event
  • Go Shopping
